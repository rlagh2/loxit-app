Loxit

Privacy Policy

The short version: Loxit does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

Loxit is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your thoughts and reminders are personal, and they should stay that way.

Data Collection

Loxit does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up required
  • No personal information collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No third-party analytics services
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No cookies or similar technologies

Data Storage

All data you create in Loxit—your reminders, notes, and any other content—is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any external servers.

Your data is stored using Apple's standard on-device storage mechanisms and is protected by your device's security features, including encryption and passcode/Face ID/Touch ID protection.

Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share. Your information is never sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties.

Widgets

Loxit offers home screen, lock screen, and Control Center widgets. These widgets access your locally stored data to display your reminders. This data never leaves your device.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual items by swiping to delete within the app
  • Clear all completed items using the "Clear All" button
  • Remove all data by deleting the app from your device

When you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

Loxit does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
